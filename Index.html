Touchless Tech for Work: Adapting to the Latest Tech Trends

Touchless technology, using sensors, cameras, and AI, enhances workplace safety and efficiency by reducing physical contact. Though costly to implement and requiring staff training, it’s increasingly essential as tech advances in AI and smart devices.
